About Me

Hi! My name is Deeya Thakur. Welcome to my portfolio website. I am a second year student studying Communication Design at the Innovative School of Design and Innovation, Mumbai. This website was made as a part of my course on the Fundamentals of Digital Prototyping, to understand the basics of web design and coding using HTML, CSS, Javascript and Bootstrap.

I'm a person who likes to experiment with design and this website was an attempt at personalising a website according to my style, that would also reflect who I am as a person and designer.

MY PROJECTS

Skillbox App Redesign

This project focused on understanding the basics of UI/UX design and mainly understanding the layouts and flow of pages in an app. I chose to redesign the Skillbox app, which is an event ticketing app. I chose this app because I personally felt that the apps original layout was a bit confusing and there was a lot of stylistic potential for the app. I chose to also incorporate elements like an article section to make the app more interesting and interactive for its target userbase.

Through the process of redesigning, some key learnings I made were on the fundamentals of UI design, maintaining a cohesive design languange and nailing details like margin spaces and correct sizes for final display.

Pinterest Login Recreation

For this project the main idea was to understand the login process of an application. I chose to recreate the login of Pinterest as I felt it was very direct and to the point, and would be a helpful exploration in understanding the user layouts.

The key learnings from this was now in the fundamentals of the User Experience, and layouting. Exploring more with the figma prototyping features and seeing how the pages are connected and how the flow between works.
